Top Destinations for this Season New

Arugambay

known locally as "Arugam Kudah"

Arugam Bay is situated on the Indian Ocean in the dry zone of Sri Lanka's southeast coast, and a historic settlement of the ancient Batticaloa Territory.

Adam's Peak

known locally as "Sripada"

Adam's Peak is a 2,243 m tall conical sacred mountain located in central Sri Lanka. The Sri Pada, a 1.8 m rock formation near the summit whose name is also used for the mountain itself.

Ella

A small town in Badulla District.

It is approximately 200 kilometres east of Colombo and is situated at an elevation of 1,041 metres above sea level. The area has a rich bio-diversity, dense with numerous varieties of flora and fauna.
